Responsive Typography

This is a demo article showing how variable fonts can adjust width dynamically based on viewport size. As the viewport shrinks, the font width becomes narrower, optimizing typographic space usage.

In addition to their technical benefits, variable fonts offer much more flexibility for typesetting in both printed and digital media. This flexibility is especially beneficial in the context of responsive design where the presentation of content adapts to different formats and contexts. The most common considerations for designing a responsive composition are tied to the availability of space.
